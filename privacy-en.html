Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

1. Introduction

At Giysene ("we", "us", or "our"), we place great importance on protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and how you are protected when you use the Giysene mobile application ("App").

Giysene is an AI-powered wardrobe management and outfit recommendation app. By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Data We Collect

The following personal data may be collected when you use the App:

  • Account Information: Email address, name (provided via Apple Sign In)
  • Clothing Photos: Images of clothing items you add to your wardrobe
  • Style Preferences: Your selected clothing style, color preferences, and style information
  • Body Type Selection: Body type information you select for outfit recommendations
  • AI Chat Messages: Content of conversations with the AI assistant
  • Coarse Location: Approximate location data used for weather-based outfit suggestions
  • Usage Data: In-app interactions and preferences

3. Purpose of Data Use

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account
  • To store and manage your wardrobe digitally
  • To provide AI-powered personalized outfit recommendations
  • To improve our recommendations based on your style preferences
  • To enhance app performance and user experience
  • To identify and resolve technical issues

4. Third-Party Services

The following third-party services are used in the App:

  • Supabase: Used for user authentication, database management, and image storage. Your data is securely stored on Supabase cloud servers. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Claude API: Used for AI-powered outfit recommendations and chat functionality. Your chat messages and style information are sent to Anthropic servers to generate recommendations. Your clothing photos are NOT sent to the AI; they remain on your device and in Supabase storage only. Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Apple Sign In: Used for secure sign-in. Apple shares limited information during authentication. Apple Privacy Policy

5. Data Storage and Security

Your personal data is stored on the Supabase cloud infrastructure. Your clothing photos are stored in Supabase Storage. All data transfers are protected with SSL/TLS encryption.

We implement industry-standard security measures to ensure the safety of your data. However, please note that no method of data transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. User Rights

You have the following rights:

  • Right of Access: You have the right to learn what personal data of yours is being processed.
  • Right to Rectification: You may request the correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to Erasure: You may request the permanent deletion of your account and all associated data (including photos, preferences, and chat history).
  • Data Portability: You may request a copy of your personal data.

When you delete your account, all your personal data, clothing photos, and chat history are permanently removed from our systems.

7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you become aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

8. Cookies and Tracking

The Giysene mobile app does not use cookies. There are no third-party advertising tracking tools.

9. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When changes are made, we will publish the updated policy within the app and on this page. You will be notified of significant changes.
